These bar charts display the estimated impact at the model horizon of each TPMA on avoiding activity.
However they do not account for interaction effects between TPMAs caused by overlaps between some activity avoidance TPMA cohorts.
This means that the impact attributed to each individual mitigator may be slightly overstated.
The overall size of this interaction effect can be viewed in the 'step_counts' sheet of the Excel download of the results (see 'Downloads' in the sidebar).
